Frigid14 (referred to as frigid) is a fork for Space Station 14, a recreation of the cult-classic Space Station 13 focusing on zombie survival inspired by games such as Project Zomboid

We differ from the regular "do your job and watch the station crumble" gameplay and instead pit players into tense situations either against AI controlled zombies or each other.

Loot houses, kill zombies, gang up with others or raid bases, just remember to wear a coat; it's fairly cold here.
